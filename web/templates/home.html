Three Ways to Model Your Data

Choose the mode that fits your experimental structure.

Experimental Uncertainty

Designed for datasets with replicate measurements. Groups rows by condition, then fits a GP on the per-group mean or standard deviation.

Experimental Data

Fits the GP directly to each observation without grouping. Best for datasets where every row is a distinct data point and no replicates exist.

Both Modes

Runs both pipelines simultaneously for side-by-side comparison. See how replicate-aggregated and row-level modeling differ on the same dataset.
